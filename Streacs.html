About project

Objective

STREACS aims to enhance Armenia’s research capacity to accelerate the country’s transition toward clean and renewable energy, while ensuring energy security. The project supports the implementation of the European Green Deal by promoting a sustainable, decarbonised, and resilient energy system adapted to Armenia’s specific geopolitical and technical context.

.

Impact

By strengthening research capacity and fostering cooperation, STREACS contributes to Armenia’s long-term decarbonisation and energy transition goals. It enables better integration of Armenia into the European Research Area and enhances its ability to produce innovative, sustainable energy solutions through shared expertise and best practices.

Our Role

STREACS facilitates the transfer of knowledge and exchange of best practices between research institutions, universities, and partners across the EU. The project builds networks for academic and policy collaboration, supporting Armenia in developing clean-energy research and innovation aligned with European standards.
